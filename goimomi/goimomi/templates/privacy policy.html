Cookie Policy

We are committed to transparency about how we use cookies on our website. Cookies help deliver a more efficient and personalized browsing experience.

What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by the browser. Cookies can remember preferences, session state, and other useful information to enhance your experience.

  • Session Cookies: temporary cookies erased when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: stored for a set time to help us analyze usage patterns and improve our services.

Cookie Categories

Essential
Required for core website functions, such as session management and shopping cart contents.
Functional
Remember user preferences and improve site usability.
Analytics
Used to measure performance and gather anonymized usage data (e.g., Google Analytics). See Google's partners privacy info: google.com/policies/privacy/partners.
Advertising
Used by us and trusted partners to provide relevant ads. We limit third-party cookies to reputable partners only.

Managing Cookies

To manage or delete cookies, please change settings in your browser. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via Settings > Privacy & Security. You may also use browser extensions to control cookies more granularly.

If you have questions about our privacy practices or want to request deletion of personal data, contact us at privacy@goimomi.example.
